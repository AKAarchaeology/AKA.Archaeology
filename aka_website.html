A.K.A Archaeology

Who We Are

A.K.A Archaeology is dedicated to preserving Arctic cultural heritage through sustainable development and advanced archaeological research. Our mission is to protect Arctic artifacts and ecosystems while promoting awareness of the Arctic's rich history.

What We Want to Accomplish

Our pilot project is focused on tokenizing Arctic artifacts and natural resources using blockchain technology. This will create a sustainable funding model for future Arctic excavations and research while preserving cultural heritage.

Challenges We Face

The Arctic is rapidly melting due to climate change, leading to the destruction and loss of invaluable artifacts. Additionally, there is a shortage of skilled Arctic archaeologists, which makes it even more difficult to protect and recover these precious historical items.
